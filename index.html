Maryland Beekeepers Association: A Website Redesign

Established in 1908, the MD Beekeeper Association has been dedicated to advocating beekeeping best practices and fostering education within their community. However, their current website lacks user-friendliness, potentially hindering community engagement and volunteer participation. As an essential organization promoting environmental stewardship, enhancing their online presence is vital to attract and retain more individuals interested in supporting their cause.
